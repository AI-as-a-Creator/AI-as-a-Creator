Young Artist

Prompt: “Write a 3 verse poem about a boy who would recreate any painting perfectly two times a day.”

Noble

Finnish Girl

Prompt: “Write a limmerick about a girl from Finland who liked to recreate any painting perfectly.”

93% Break

Prompt: “Write a 3 verse poem about a boy who would predict the outcome of a snooker match with 93% accuracy seven times a day.”

Summer’s Glow

Prompt: “Write a haiku about seven humming birds.”

Success Rate

Prompt: “Write a limmerick about a girl from at the bottom of the garden who liked to predict the outcome of a snooker match with 93% accuracy.”

Making Scents

Alive

Brand New Spring

Green Apples
